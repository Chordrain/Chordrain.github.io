# nihilistic

* holding or showing the belief that life has no meaning or purpose and that religious and moral principles have no value 虚无主义的

>Her latest play is a nihilistic vision of the world of the future.
>
>她最新的话剧展现了未来世界的虚无景象。

# nihilism

* **(philosophy 哲)** the belief that nothing has any value, especially that religious and moral principles have no value 虚无主义
