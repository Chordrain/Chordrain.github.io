#English #words 

# dwindle

* **dwindle (away) (to sth)** to become gradually less or smaller （逐渐）减少，变小，缩小

>Support for the party has dwindled away to nothing.
>
>支持这个党派的人渐渐化为乌有。

# dwindling

* 这个单词可以作为 increasing 的反义词来使用
