# dismiss

* **dismiss sb/sth (as sth)** to decide that sb/sth is not important and not worth thinking or talking about 不予考虑；摒弃；对…不屑一提

>Vegetarians are no longer dismissed as cranks.
>
>素食主义者不再被当作怪人。

* **dismiss sth (from sth)** to put thoughts or feelings out of your mind 去除，消除，摒除（思想、感情等）
* synonym：dispense

>He dismissed her from his mind.
>
>他挥去了对她的思念。

* **dimiss sb (from sth)** to officially remove sb from their job 解雇；免职；开除

>She claims she was unfairly dismissed from her post.
>
>她声称自己被无理免职。

* to send sb away or allow them to leave 让（某人）离开；把（某人）打发走；解散

>At 12 o'clock the class was dismissed.
>
>12 点下课了。
