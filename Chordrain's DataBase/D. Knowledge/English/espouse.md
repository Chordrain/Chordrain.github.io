#English #words 

# espouse

* [transitive] **(formal)** to give your support to a belief, policy etc. 支持，拥护，赞成（信仰、政策等）

>They espoused the notion of equal opportunity for all in education. 
>
>他们赞同在教育方面人人机会均等的观念。
