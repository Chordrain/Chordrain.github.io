# backwater

* a part of a river away from the main part, where the water only moves slowly （河流的）滞水，壅水，回水

* a place that is away from the places where most things happen, and is therefore not affected by events, progress, new ideas, etc. 与世隔绝的（或缺乏生气的、落后的）地区

* a place or condition in which no development or progress is taking place 落后地区；停滞状态

>The country remained an economic backwater.
>
>该国经济仍然停滞不前，一潭死水。
