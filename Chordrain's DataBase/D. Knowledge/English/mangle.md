# mangle

* to crush or twist sth so that it is badly damaged 压碎；撕烂；严重损坏

>His hand was mangled in the machine. 他的手卷到机器里轧烂了。

* to spoil sth, for example a poem or a piece of music, by saying it wrongly or playing it badly 糟蹋（如蹩脚的诗朗诵或拙劣的演奏）
* Synonym：ruin

* ( also **wring·er** ) a machine with two **rollers** used especially in the past for squeezing the water out of clothes that had been washed （尤指旧时的）轧布机，轧液机
