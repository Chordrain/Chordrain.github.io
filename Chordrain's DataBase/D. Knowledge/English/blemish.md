# blemish

* [countable] a mark on the skin or on an object that spoils it and makes it look less beautiful or perfect 斑点；疤痕；瑕疵

>His reputation is without a blemish.
>
>他的名誉可说是白璧无瑕。

* [usually passive, transitive] (formal) to spoil sth that is beautiful or perfect in all other ways 破坏…的完美；玷污

>He wasn't about to blemish that pristine record.  
>
>他不想破坏那完美的记录。
