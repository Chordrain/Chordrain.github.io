#English #words 

disenchant 这个单词较为复杂，能讲的东西比较多，我们从其词根 chant 开始讲起。

# chant

* ​[countable] words or phrases that a group of people shout or sing again and again 反复呼喊的话语；重复唱的歌词

>The crowd broke into chants of 'Out! Out!'.
>
>人群突然爆发出“下台！下台！”的反复呼喊声。

* [countable, uncountable] a religious song or prayer or a way of singing, using only a few notes that are repeated many times 圣歌；反复吟咏的祷文；单调的吟唱

>A Buddhist chant
>
>佛经诵念

* to sing or shout the same words or phrases many times 反复唱；反复呼喊

>The crowd chanted their hero's name.
>
>人群不断地呼唤着自己英雄的名字。

# enchant

* to attract sb strongly and make them feel very interested, excited, etc. 使着迷；使陶醉

>Prima donna, enchant us once again!  
>
>首席女高音，请再次让我们痴迷！

* to place sb/sth under a magic **spell** (= magic words that have special powers) 使中魔法；对…用魔法（或念咒语）

>Can the wizard enchant Ford instead? 
>
>魔术师是否能施法于福特？

# disenchant

* to make disappointed or disillusioned 使失望; 使幻想破灭
* 这个单词，或者说其主动用法的使用频率较低，一般使用其被动形式和形容词 disenchanted

# disenchanted

* [adj.] **be disenchanted (with sb/sth)** no longer feeling enthusiasm for sb/sth; not believing sth is good or worth doing 不再着迷的；不再抱幻想的
* Synonym：[[disillusion|disillusioned]]

>He was becoming disenchanted with his job as a lawyer.
>
>他对自己的律师工作渐渐地不抱幻想了。

>这里不得不提到 My Chemical Romance 的经典之作 *Disenchanted*，非常好听的情绪摇滚
