# cleavage

* [uncountable] the space between a woman's breasts that can be seen above a dress that does not completely cover them (妇女穿低胸服时露出的) 乳沟

>She leaned forward slightly, revealing a deep cleavage.
>
>她身体微微前倾，露出一条深深的乳沟。

* a difference or division between people or groups (个人或集团之间的) 差异，差别，分歧

>a deep cleavage between rich and poor in society
>
>贫富社会的鸿沟
