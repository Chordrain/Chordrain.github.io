# snuff

* [verb] **snuff sth (out)** to stop a small flame from burning, especially by pressing it between your fingers or covering it with sth 掐灭，闷熄，熄灭 (小火苗)
* snuff sth out 这个词组的引申义为“扼杀；消灭”

>An innocent child's life has been snuffed out by this senseless shooting.
>
>这胡乱一枪就要了一个无辜孩子的命。

* [verb] (动物) to smell sth by breathing in noisily through the nose 出声地嗅

>The dogs were snuffing gently at my feet. 几只狗轻轻地嗅着我的脚。

* [uncountable] in the form of a powder that people take by breathing it into their noses **tobacco** 鼻烟
