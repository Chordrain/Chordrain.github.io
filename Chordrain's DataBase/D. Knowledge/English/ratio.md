# ratio

* **ratio (of A to B)** the relationship between two groups of people or things that is represented by two numbers showing how much larger one group is than the other 比率；比例
* 复数形式：ratios

>What is the ratio of men to women in the department?
>
>这个部门的男女比例是多少？

>The school has a very high teacher-student ratio.
>
>这所学校的师生比例很高。
