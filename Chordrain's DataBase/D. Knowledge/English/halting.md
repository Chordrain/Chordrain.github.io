#English #words 

# halting

* [adjective] stopping and starting often, especially because you are not certain or are not very confident 断断续续的；结结巴巴的

>In a halting voice she said that she wished to make a statement.
>
>她结结巴巴地说自己要做一项声明。

# halt

* [transitive, intransitive] to stop; to make sb/sth stop （使）停止，停下

>She walked towards him and then halted.
>
>她向他走去，然后停下。

>The police were halting traffic on the parade route.
>
>警察正阻止车辆在游行路线上行驶。
