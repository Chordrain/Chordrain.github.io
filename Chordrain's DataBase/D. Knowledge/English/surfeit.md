#English #words 

# surfeit

* [countable] **sufeit (of sth)** an amount that is too large 过量
* Synonym：excess

>Indigestion can be brought on by a surfeit of rich food.
>
>消化不良可能是由于摄入过量的高脂肪食物引起的。
