# lift 的其他用法

life 除了常见的“举起；鼓舞兴致；提高”之外，还有很多鲜为人知的意思。

* to remove or end restrictions 撤销限制/规则

>Martial law has now been lifted.
>
>戒严令现已解除。

* (云、雾等) to rise and disappear 消散；消失

>The fog began to lift.
>
>雾开始散了。

* to use sb's ideas or words without asking permission or without saying where they come from 剽窃；盗用；抄袭

>She lifted most of the ideas from a book she had been reading.
>
>大部分观点都是她从一直在看的一本书里抄来的。
