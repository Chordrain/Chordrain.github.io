#English #words 

# accrue

* [transitive] **accrue (to sb) (from sth)** to increase over a period of time (逐渐) 增长，增加

>economic benefits accruing to the country from tourism
>
>旅游业为该国带来的经济效益

* to allow a sum of money or debts to grow over a period of time (使钱款、债务) 积累

>The firm had accrued debts of over $6m.
>
>该公司已积欠了600多万元的债务。
