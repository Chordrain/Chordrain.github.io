#English #words 

# heavy-handed

* not showing a sympathetic understanding of the feelings of other people 缺乏同情心的；冷酷的

>a heavy-handed approach 冷酷无情的方法

* using unnecessary force 强暴的；高压的

>heavy-handed police methods 警察的高压手段

* using too much of sth in a way that can cause damage 用某物过多的；大手大脚的
* 常用搭配：**be heavy-handed with**

>Don't be too heavy-handed with the salt.
>
>别放太多的盐。
