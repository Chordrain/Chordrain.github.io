#English #words 

# gore

* [uncountable] thick blood that has flowed from a wound, especially in a violent situation (尤指在暴力情况下) 伤口流出的血，凝固的血

>The movie is not just **blood and gore** _(= scenes of violence)_ ; it has a thrilling story.
>
>这部影片不光有血淋淋的暴力场面，还有扣人心弦的情节。

* (被动物用角或长牙) 顶伤，戳伤

>He was gored by a bull.
>
>他被公牛顶伤。
