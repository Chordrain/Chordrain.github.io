#English #words 

# soothe

* to make sb who is anxious, upset, etc. feel calmer 安慰；抚慰；劝慰
* Synonym：calm

>The music soothed her for a while.
>
>音乐让她稍微安静了一会儿。

* to make a **tense** or painful part of your body feel more comfortable 减轻，缓解，缓和（身体某部位的紧张或疼痛）
* Synonym：relieve

>Take a warm bath to soothe tense, tired muscles.
>
>洗个热水澡，让紧张疲劳的肌肉放松一下。
