# shading

* [uncountable] the use of colour, pencil lines, etc. to give an impression of light and shade in a picture or to emphasize areas of a map, diagram, etc. （绘画的）明暗法；（地图、图表等中）颜色浓淡强调某些部分的运用

* _shadings_ [ pl.] slight differences that exist between different aspects of the same thing （同一事物不同层面之间的）细微差别
