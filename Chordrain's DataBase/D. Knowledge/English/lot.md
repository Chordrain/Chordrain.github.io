# lot

* _the lot, the whole lot_ [ sing.+sing./pl.v.] ( __informal__ ) the whole number or amount of people or things 全体；全部；整个

>Get out of my house, **the lot of you**!
>
>你们别待在我家里，通通给我滚出去！

* [C+sing./pl.v.] (_especially BrE_) a group or set of people or things （一）组，群，批，套

>The first lot of visitors has/have arrived.
>
>首批游客已经到达。

* [ sing.] a person's luck or situation in life 命运；生活状况
* Synonym：destiny

>She was feeling dissatisfied with her lot.
>
>她对自己的命运感到不满。
