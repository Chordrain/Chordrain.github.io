#English #words 

# cram

  * **cram (sth) into/onto sth~ (sth)** into push or force sb/sth into a small space; to move into a small space with the result that it is full 把…塞进；挤满；塞满

>He crammed eight people into his car.
>
>他往他的车里硬塞进八个人。

* [intransitive] **cram (for sth) (rather old-fashioned)** to learn a lot of things in a short time, in preparation for an exam (为应考) 临时死记硬背

>He's been cramming for his exams all week.
>
>他整个星期都一直在拼命准备应考。
