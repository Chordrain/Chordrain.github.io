#English #words 

# draconian

* [adjective] (of a law, punishment, etc. 法律、惩罚等) extremely cruel and severe 德拉古式的；严酷的；残忍的

>So far the European response has been less draconian than many feared. 
>
>目前为止欧洲的应对没有像人们想的那样严酷。

>ORIGIN 词源
>
>From **Draco** , a legislator in ancient Athens who gave severe punishments for crimes, especially the punishment of being killed. 源自古雅典立法者德拉古，他严厉惩治犯罪，甚至施以死刑。
