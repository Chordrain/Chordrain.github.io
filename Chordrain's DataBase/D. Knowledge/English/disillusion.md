#English #words 

# disillusion

* [transitive] **disillusion sb** to destroy sb's belief in or good opinion of sb/sth 使醒悟；使不再抱幻想；使理想破灭

>I hate to disillusion you, but not everyone is as honest as you.
>
>我实在不愿把实情告诉你，但并非人人都像你那样诚实。

* [uncountable, singular] **disillusion (with something)** the state of being disillusioned

>There is growing disillusion with the political system.
>
>人民对政治制度的美化幻想逐渐破灭。

# disillusioned

- 由其过去分词可派生出 disillusioned 这个形容词，意为“adj. 大失所望的；不再抱幻想的；幻想破灭的”。
- Synonym：[[disenchanted|disenchanted]]
- 常用搭配：**be illusioned with sth**

>I soon became disillusioned with the job.
>
>我不久便对这个工作不再抱幻想了。
