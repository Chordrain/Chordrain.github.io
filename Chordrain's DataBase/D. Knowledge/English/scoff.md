#English #words 

# scoff

* [intransitive, transitive] to talk about somebody/something in a way that makes it clear that you think they are stupid or silly 嘲笑，嘲弄
* 常用搭配：**scoff at somebody/something**

>He scoffed at our amateurish attempts.
