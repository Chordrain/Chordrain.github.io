#English #words 

# rampant

* [adjective] **(坏事)** existing or spreading everywhere in a way that cannot be controlled 泛滥的；猖獗的

>Unemployment is now rampant in most of Europe.
>
>在欧洲的大部分地区，失业问题难以控制。

* [adjective] **(植物)** growing thickly and very fast in a way that cannot be controlled 疯长的
